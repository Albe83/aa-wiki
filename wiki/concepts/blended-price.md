# Blended Price

Combined price used by Artificial Analysis to facilitate comparison between models, calculated assuming a **7:2:1** ratio of:

- **Cache hit** (7 parts): tokens already cached, discounted price
- **Input** (2 parts): input tokens not cached, full price
- **Output** (1 part): tokens generated by the model
