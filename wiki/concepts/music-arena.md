# AA Music Arena

Blind pairwise listening arena for evaluating music generation quality. Users are presented with two anonymized music tracks and vote on which they prefer. Elo scores are derived via Bradley-Terry MLE with 95% confidence intervals. The arena covers **two modalities**: Instrumental and Vocals. Voters must listen to at least **10 seconds of each track** before casting a vote, ensuring meaningful comparisons.

All tracks undergo rigorous audio normalization: loudness normalized to -16 LUFS (ITU-R BS.1770) with a -1 dBTP peak ceiling, no compression or limiting applied. Output is standardized to 320 kbps MP3 at 44.1 kHz with all metadata stripped to prevent format-related confounds.

## Key details

### Elo Calculation
- Bradley-Terry MLE with 95% confidence intervals
- Separate per modality (Instrumental, Vocals)
- Global Elo aggregates all eligible votes

### Voting Rules
- Blind pairwise comparison of anonymized tracks
- Must listen to ≥10 seconds of each track before voting
- Personal leaderboard unlocks after minimum votes — shows single user's preference rankings vs. global consensus

### Audio Normalization Pipeline
- **Loudness:** -16 LUFS target (ITU-R BS.1770) with -1 dBTP peak ceiling
- **Processing:** No compression or limiting — purely level normalization
- **Output format:** 320 kbps MP3, 44.1 kHz
- **Metadata:** Stripped from all tracks to prevent format confounds

### Leaderboards
- **Global:** Aggregates all eligible votes across all users
- **Personal:** Single user's votes only (unlocks after minimum vote threshold)
- Both filterable by genre: Pop, Hip-Hop, EDM, Classical, Jazz & Blues

### Generation Defaults
- 1 track per prompt, approximately 3 minutes duration
- Seed: 42
- Vocals: lyrics generated by the model from the prompt (not pre-written)
- Metadata stripped from all output files

### Independence
No compensation accepted from providers.
