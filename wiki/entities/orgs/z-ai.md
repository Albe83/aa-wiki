# Z AI

Spin-off from Zhipu AI, based in Beijing. Creator of the GLM (General Language Model) series, using a unique architecture distinct from standard decoder-only transformers. Focused on bilingual Chinese-English enterprise AI.

## Key facts

- GLM architecture is distinct from the standard GPT-style decoder-only design
- Originated from Tsinghua University NLP research
- Zhipu AI is one of China's "AI Tigers" alongside Moonshot AI and MiniMax
- Z AI was spun off as a separate entity around 2025
