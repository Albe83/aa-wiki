# Alibaba Cloud (Qwen)

The cloud computing arm of Alibaba Group, based in Hangzhou. Creator of the Qwen model family (Tongyi Qianwen), offering both open-weight and proprietary LLMs. The largest cloud provider in China and Asia-Pacific.

## Key facts

- Qwen models are among the most popular open-weight alternatives globally
- Operates both as a Model Creator (Qwen) and a cloud Provider for third-party models
- Tongyi Lab develops the underlying AI research
- Strong multimodal capabilities across text, image, and code
