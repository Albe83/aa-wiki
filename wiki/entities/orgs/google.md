# Google DeepMind

Google's consolidated AI research organization, formed by merging DeepMind and Google Brain in 2023. Creator of the Gemini model family, TPU hardware, and the Vertex AI platform.

- Natively multimodal models (Gemini) — text, image, audio, video, code
- Custom TPU hardware gives it infrastructure independence from NVIDIA
- Gemma open-weight models compete with Llama and Mistral
- AA's primary testing server is Google Cloud us-central1-a
