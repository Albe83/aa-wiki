# Anthropic

AI research lab and Public Benefit Corporation, creator of the Claude model family. Founded by former OpenAI executives Dario and Daniela Amodei. Emphasizes AI safety and constitutional AI methods.

## Key facts

- One of the three main proprietary frontier model labs alongside OpenAI and Google
- Claude models are available on multiple providers (Anthropic API, AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex AI)
- Structured as a Public Benefit Corporation with a Long-Term Benefit Trust overseeing the board
- Valued at ~$965B as of May 2026
