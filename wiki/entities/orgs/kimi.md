# Moonshot AI (Kimi)

Beijing-based Chinese LLM lab, creator of the Kimi model series. Known for long-context reasoning capabilities and competitive performance in Chinese and English.

## Key facts

- Kimi K3 and K2.5 are notable for their long context window capabilities
- Founded by Yang Zhilin (Tsinghua University / CMU)
- One of China's leading AI startups alongside Zhipu AI, MiniMax, and 01.AI
- Competes with DeepSeek and Qwen in the Chinese LLM market
