# MBZUAI Institute of Foundation Models

The research institute within Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) in Abu Dhabi — the world's first graduate-level AI university. Focuses on foundation model research with emphasis on multilingual and Arabic applications.
