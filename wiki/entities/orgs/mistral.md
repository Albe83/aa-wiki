# Mistral AI

French AI lab based in Paris, creator of competitive open-weight LLMs. Founded by ex-Meta AI and Google DeepMind researchers. A leading European AI company emphasizing open-weight releases.

## Key facts

- Produces both open-weight (Mistral, Mixtral) and proprietary (Mistral Large) models
- Valued at ~$14 billion, ~350 employees
- Le Chat is its consumer chat product and API platform
- Emphasizes European AI sovereignty as part of its mission
