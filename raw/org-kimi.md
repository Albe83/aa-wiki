# Moonshot AI (Kimi)

- **Headquarters:** Beijing, China
- **Founded:** 2023
- **Founder:** Yang Zhilin (杨植麟)
- **Key models:** Kimi K3, Kimi K2.5 (long-context LLMs)
- **Focus:** Chinese LLM lab, long-context reasoning, large-scale AI
- **Notable:** Yang Zhilin studied at Carnegie Mellon and Tsinghua. Kimi K3 and K2.5 are notable for their long context window capabilities. Moonshot AI is one of China's leading AI startups.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moonshot_AI
