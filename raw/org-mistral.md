# Mistral AI

- **Headquarters:** Paris, France
- **Founded:** April 2023
- **Founders:** Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, Timothée Lacroix
- **Key models:** Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B, Mistral Large, Le Chat
- **Valuation:** ~$14 billion (2025)
- **Employees:** ~350
- **Focus:** Open-weight LLMs, European AI sovereignty, multimodal models
- **Notable:** Founders previously worked at Meta AI (FAIR) and Google DeepMind. Known for early open-weight releases that competed with larger proprietary models.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mistral_AI
