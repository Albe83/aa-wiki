# MiniMax

- **Headquarters:** Shanghai, China
- **Founded:** 2021
- **Founders:** Yan Junjie, Yang Bin
- **Key models:** MiniMax-Text-01 (language), MiniMax-Image-01, MiniMax-Speech
- **Focus:** Chinese AI lab, multimodal models spanning text, image, and speech generation
- **Notable:** One of China's "AI Tigers" alongside Moonshot AI, Zhipu AI, Baichuan, and 01.AI. Strong vertical in speech technology.
