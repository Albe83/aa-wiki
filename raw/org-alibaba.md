# Alibaba Cloud (Qwen)

- **Headquarters:** Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China
- **Founded:** 2009 (Cloud division), Qwen team within Tongyi Lab
- **Key models:** Qwen family (Qwen2.5, Qwen3), Tongyi Qianwen (通义千问)
- **Focus:** Chinese cloud provider, open-weight and proprietary LLMs, multimodal models
- **Notable:** Qwen models are widely used as open-weight alternatives. Alibaba Cloud is the largest cloud provider in China and Asia-Pacific.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alibaba_Cloud
