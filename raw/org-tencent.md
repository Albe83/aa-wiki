# Tencent AI

- **Headquarters:** Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
- **Founded:** 1998 (Tencent), AI lab expanded ~2016
- **Key models:** Hy LLM series (HunYuan, Hy3, etc.)
- **Focus:** Chinese tech conglomerate, LLMs for WeChat/QQ ecosystem, cloud AI services
- **Notable:** Tencent is one of the world's largest tech companies by market cap. The Hy (HunYuan) models power AI features across Tencent's vast consumer and enterprise platforms.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tencent
