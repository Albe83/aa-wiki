# Z AI (Zhipu AI spin-off)

- **Headquarters:** Beijing, China
- **Founded:** 2025 (as spin-off from Zhipu AI)
- **Key models:** GLM series (GLM-4, ChatGLM)
- **Focus:** Chinese LLM lab, bilingual (Chinese-English) models, enterprise AI
- **Notable:** Zhipu AI originated from Tsinghua University research. Z AI is a recent spin-off entity. GLM models use a unique architecture (General Language Model) distinct from standard decoder-only transformers.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhipu_AI
