# Music Generation Benchmarking Methodology

**Source:** https://artificialanalysis.ai/music/methodology
**Fetched:** 2026-07-21

## Scope

Benchmarks music generation models via human preference voting in Music Arena. Two modalities: Instrumental, Vocals.

## Quality Elo

From blind pairwise votes in Music Arena (Bradley-Terry MLE). 95% CI reported. Requirements: listen ≥10 seconds of each track before voting.

### Audio Normalization
- Loudness: -16 LUFS target (ITU-R BS.1770), -1 dBTP peak ceiling, no compression/limiting
- Format: 320 kbps MP3, 44.1 kHz, metadata stripped

### Leaderboards
- Global: aggregates all eligible votes
- Personal: single user's votes (unlocks after minimum votes)
- Both filterable by genre (Pop, Hip-Hop, EDM, Classical, Jazz & Blues)

## Default Settings

- 1 track per prompt, ~3 minutes, MP3 format, 44.1 kHz, seed 42
- Vocals: lyrics generated by model from prompt

## Independence

No compensation from providers.
