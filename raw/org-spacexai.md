# xAI

- **Headquarters:** Memphis, Tennessee, USA
- **Founded:** July 2023
- **Founder:** Elon Musk
- **Key models:** Grok series (Grok-1, Grok-2, Grok-3)
- **Infrastructure:** Colossus supercomputer (Memphis), one of the world's largest GPU clusters
- **Focus:** "Understand the true nature of the universe"; competitive frontier LLMs
- **Notable:** Musk also co-founded OpenAI. Grok models emphasize personality and real-time knowledge via X/Twitter integration. Rapid scaling with massive compute investment.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XAI_(company)
