# Anthropic

- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, California, USA
- **Founded:** 2021
- **Founders:** Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei
- **Key models:** Claude (Claude Opus, Claude Sonnet, Claude Haiku)
- **Valuation:** ~$965 billion (May 2026)
- **Employees:** ~2,500
- **Focus:** AI safety, constitutional AI, frontier LLMs
- **Notable:** Dario Amodei was formerly VP of Research at OpenAI. The company is structured as a Public Benefit Corporation (PBC) with a Long-Term Benefit Trust.
