# Google DeepMind

- **Headquarters:** London, UK (DeepMind) / Mountain View, California, USA (Google)
- **Founded:** 2010 (DeepMind), acquired by Google 2014; merged with Google Brain 2023
- **Key people:** Demis Hassabis (CEO), Jeff Dean (Chief Scientist)
- **Key models:** Gemini family (Ultra, Pro, Flash, Nano), Gemma open models, Imagen (image generation), Veo (video generation), Chirp (speech)
- **Infrastructure:** Custom TPU (Tensor Processing Unit) hardware, Vertex AI platform
- **Notable:** DeepMind produced AlphaGo, AlphaFold. Gemini models are natively multimodal.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google_DeepMind
