# MBZUAI Institute of Foundation Models

- **Headquarters:** Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
- **Founded:** 2019 (MBZUAI university), Foundation Models institute established ~2024
- **Key models:** Jais (Arabic-English bilingual LLM), Falcon series contributions
- **Focus:** Academic AI research, foundation models for multilingual and especially Arabic applications
- **Notable:** Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) is the world's first graduate-level AI university. The Institute of Foundation Models focuses on open-weight model research.
