# NVIDIA

- **Headquarters:** Santa Clara, California, USA
- **Founded:** 1993
- **CEO:** Jensen Huang
- **Key products:** H100/H200/B100/B200 GPUs, GB200 NVL72, CUDA ecosystem, Nemotron models
- **Focus:** World leader in AI GPU hardware, inference infrastructure, and AI software stack
- **Notable:** Supplies the vast majority of AI training and inference GPUs globally. Also develops its own AI models (Nemotron). Dominant position in the AI hardware supply chain.
- **Source:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nvidia
